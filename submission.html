Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers addressing significant challenges and solutions in the design, development, and implementation of intelligent and sustainable AI systems.

Submission Tracks

Track 1: Intelligent Systems & Architectures

Track 2: Sustainability and Green AI

Track 3: Societal Impact and Human-centric AI

Paper Submission Guidelines
  • Papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • Manuscripts should be prepared in English.
  • Length: 6 to 8 pages (including figures and references)
